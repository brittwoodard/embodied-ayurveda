You’ve studied the texts and watched the videos—now, move beyond the screen. This immersion is designed to bridge the gap between academic study and the tactile reality of being a practitioner.

The heart of this experience is the opportunity to practice under the direct guidance of a skilled mentor. In our daily workshops, you won’t just learn a protocol; you’ll have your hand placement corrected, your questions answered in real-time, and your diagnostic skills refined by an Ayurvedic Doctor with years of clinical wisdom.

Settle into our quiet villa and allow your knowledge to settle into your muscle memory. This is where theory finally meets the artistry of hands-on practice.

This immersion follows a "4-days on, 1-day off" rhythm, so that you have the space to integrate what you're learning with personal reflection.

  • Start each morning with grounding yoga to anchor your energy before diving into practical workshops.
  • Engage in peer assessments and practice groups to refine your diagnostic and observation skills.
  • Practice traditional therapies, like kati and janu vasti, nasya, and pada abhyanga, so that you can give them to your clients when you return home.
  • Make medicated ghees and oils, that you can use for herbal therapy, nasya, and other traditional applications.
  • Explore supportive wellness strategies under guided mentorship.
  • Live an Ayurvedic lifestyle in a stunning environment. One of the hardest parts of lifestyle is maintaining it when your friends and family are on a different journey.

Leave Bali not just with a manual, but with the confidence to start working with clients as an Ayurvedic Practitioner (even if you're still a student).
