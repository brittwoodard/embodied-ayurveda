About Embodied Ayurveda

Embodied Ayurveda was created to bring Ayurvedic education to life through travel, hands-on learning, and experiences that stay with you long after you return home. We offer immersive retreats and clinical trainings in inspiring destinations, where students and practitioners can deepen their understanding, build confidence, and reconnect with the heart of this ancient healing tradition.

This is more than just theory in a textbook. Our programs are designed for people who want to live Ayurveda, who are ready to step out of the online classroom and into real-world practice, guided by experienced teachers in beautiful places that spark curiosity and joy.

Our Ethos

At Embodied Ayurveda, everything we do is rooted in experience. We don’t just talk about transformation—we create the space for it to unfold naturally, through connection, movement, learning, and beauty. These are the values that guide how we teach, how we travel, and how we show up for our community:

Authenticity

We hold a deep respect for the roots of Ayurveda. Everything we teach is grounded in tradition. We work with trusted teachers, focus on foundational principles, and share practices that are both powerful and practical. Because Ayurveda isn’t something to sell; it’s something to embody.

Connection & Community

Healing doesn’t happen in isolation. Our programs bring together curious, kind-hearted people who are on their own paths but walking side by side. We share meals, hold space, ask questions, and laugh a lot. Whether you come alone or with a friend, you’ll leave feeling connected—to yourself, to others, and to something bigger.

Inspiring

We believe inspiration is medicine. Whether it’s learning Ayurveda through touch instead of textbooks, traveling somewhere new, or stepping into a space where you feel fully yourself—our retreats and trainings are designed to spark something inside. We want you to leave not just with knowledge, but with vision, confidence, and momentum.

Wonderlust

Travel is part of the medicine. Every location is chosen with care…places that invite you to slow down, look around, and breathe a little deeper. We’re here to experience. To fall in love with the world again. And to remember that Ayurveda is not just a science, it’s a way of seeing.
