Privacy Policy

At Embodied Ayurveda, we care deeply about your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Whether you’re joining us for a retreat, exploring our offerings, or simply browsing our site, we want you to feel safe, supported, and informed every step of the way. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with us. By using our website or participating in our programs, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for a retreat, download resources, contact us, or join our community, we may collect details like your name, email address, phone number, travel information, and other information necessary to support your experience with us.
  • Website Usage Data: Like many websites, we gather basic information about your visit — things like your IP address, browser type, and which pages you view — to help us better understand what’s helpful to you and to make our website even better.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Communicate with You: We use your information to respond to your questions, provide updates about your retreat or training, and share helpful resources and opportunities.
  • To Improve Our Services: Usage data helps us improve your experience with our website and offerings.
  • To Meet Legal Requirements: Sometimes we may need to process your information to meet legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.

Protecting Your Information

We take protecting your information seriously. While no method of online transmission or electronic storage can be 100% secure, we use appropriate security measures to safeguard your data to the best of our ability.

Sharing Your Information

We will never sell or trade your personal information. We only share your data when absolutely necessary to provide our services (for example, with a retreat venue for room arrangements) or to comply with legal obligations.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to make your experience smoother. Cookies help us understand what you find useful so we can serve you better. You can choose to adjust your browser settings if you prefer not to accept cookies.

External Links

Sometimes we share resources or partner websites we think you’ll love. Please note that we aren’t responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

Children's Privacy

Embodied Ayurveda offerings are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a child’s information has been submitted to us, please reach out — we’ll address it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in how we operate or meet new legal standards. When we do, we’ll update the date at the bottom of this page.

If you have any questions about how your information is used or if you’d just like to reach out, you can contact us.
