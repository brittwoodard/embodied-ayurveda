Join Embodied Ayurveda at the crossroads of the Landes and the Basque Country for a restorative French yoga retreat where yoga, nature, and deep rest come together in the breathtaking landscapes

Located just inland from the iconic coast of Biarritz, the hinterland of Southwest France is breathtaking. With its rolling green hills, the quiet winding waters of the Bidouze River, and the golden light of the Adour valley, this landscape has long been a secret refuge for those seeking beauty and stillness.

Here, we combine the rustic elegance of a restored 18th-century estate with the transformative science of Ayurveda. We offer carefully designed 3 day/3 night retreats...in a space to slow down, breathe deeply, and reconnect with yourself amidst seven hectares of wild meadows and ancient stone walls.

Our weekends are designed to bridge the gap between retreat and daily life. It is a space to slow down, breathe deeply, and gather the tools to thrive long after you return home.

  • Begin each day in our stunning yoga shala with a morning practice designed to ground and restore.
  • Join hands-on workshops and enjoy nourishing Ayurvedic meals around a shared table.
  • Spend your afternoons walking through the countryside, riding horses, or bonding with a community of women in our seven hectares of wild meadows.
  • Unwind in the heated pool and jacuzzi, or disconnect from your phone and curl up by the fireplace in our beautifully decorated home.

Leave the French countryside feeling lighter, nourished, and equipped with a sustainable wellness practice for life.

Set within seven hectares of wild parkland, the house is designed for silence and perspective. Every corner of the estate has been crafted by local artisans, using natural materials that allow the building to breathe. From the expansive terrace overlooking the Adour valley to the beautifully decorated nooks inside, the architecture itself invites you to slow down.

This is a place where history feels present, yet the amenities are contemporary. It is a home that feels both grand in scale and deeply private. It's the perfect container for a weekend of transformation.
